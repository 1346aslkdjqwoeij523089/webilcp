Who We Are

Illinois State Roleplay (ISRP) is a dedicated community for Emergency Response: Local Coordinator (ER:LC) players who seek a realistic, professional, and enjoyable roleplay experience. Our community was built on the principles of teamwork, respect, and dedication to the craft of roleplay.

We offer a structured environment where members can grow as roleplayers, develop their characters, and form lasting friendships with like-minded individuals. Whether you're a seasoned veteran or new to ER:LC, ISRP provides the perfect platform to enhance your skills.

Community Standards

Rules & Guidelines

Please review our community rules before joining

General Rules

Treat all community members with respect. Harassment, discrimination, and toxic behavior will not be tolerated. Maintain professionalism in all interactions.

Do not use out-of-character knowledge for in-character actions. Keep IC and OOC separate. Information learned OOC should not influence your roleplay.

Your character cannot do impossible actions. All actions must be realistic and allow for counterplay. No "I win" scenarios without proper roleplay setup.

When in-game events are happening, prioritize roleplay over everything else. Avoid distracted behavior and maintain immersion.

In-Game Rules

Actions must be realistic and follow real-world procedures. Follow traffic laws, proper arrest procedures, and emergency protocols.

Follow proper pursuit protocols. Use pit maneuvers, tire deflation devices, or boxing techniques. No ramming vehicles unless authorized.

Use force must be proportional to the threat. Follow the continuum of force. Lethal force only as a last resort when lives are in danger.

Properly secure scenes, establish perimeters, and coordinate with other units. Maintain incident command structure for larger scenes.

Discord Rules

Use appropriate channels for discussions. No spamming, flooding, or excessive caps. Keep conversations on-topic for each channel.

No NSFW, explicit, or offensive content. This includes images, links, and text. Violations result in immediate permanent ban.

No advertising other servers, communities, or services without staff permission. No self-promotion in public channels.

Use push-to-talk in busy channels. No background noise, music, or excessive side conversations. Mute when not speaking.
